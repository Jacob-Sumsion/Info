My Favorite List About Dinosaurs
  1. Tyrannosaurus Rex
    • Known for its massive size and fearsome jaws.
    • One of the most well-known and last dinosaurs to exist before the mass extinction.
  2. Velociraptor
    • Famed for its speed and intelligence.
    • Had a large curved claw on each hindfoot, probably used as a weapon.
  3. Triceratops
    • Noted for its impressive skull with two large horns and a smaller one above its nose.
    • Had a large bony frill protecting its neck.
  4. Stegosaurus
    • Known for its row of large, bony plates along its back.
    • It had a spiked tail for defense, sometimes called a "thagomizer".
  5. Brachiosaurus
    • One of the largest animals to have ever walked the Earth, known for its long neck.
    • Likely fed on high vegetation, towering over other dinosaurs.
